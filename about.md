- 1.21-1.21.3: Tricky Trials
- 1.20-1.20.6: Trails & Tales
- 1.19-1.19.4: The Wild
- 1.18-1.18.2: Caves & Cliffs II
- 1.17-1.17.1: Caves & Cliffs I
- 1.16-1.16.5: The Nether
- 1.15-1.15.2: Buzzy Bees
- 1.14-1.14.4: Village & Pillage
